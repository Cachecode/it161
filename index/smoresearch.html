Social Media Optimization

Social media are websites and apps that allow the creation and sharing of content. Platforms like, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram. According to Statista—Worldwide, there are around 3.78 billion users on social media in 2021, With a growing estimation of 4.41 billion users by 2025, to gain a real presence today, learning the ways of social media optimization is a no brainer, it is a must. Almost every business or brand’s main goal is to expand reach, generate traffic, and present their image. One of the biggest advantages of social media is that it has global reach. Unlike Social media marketing, which has to do with paid advertising, SMO is more of a focus on non-paid content.

So, how can we use social media optimization? SMO is a process in which you use social media to promote business or brands. Most social networks use hashtags; Hashtags allows users to categorize content. People use these hashtags to search for content as well. Another way to get started with SMO is to use plugins. Plugins allow others to share your content on their own social media or network, which is an easy way to implement SMO just about anywhere.

You may have heard of Search Engine Optimization (SEO). SMO and SEO have some similarities, but they are different. According to StatCounter, currently, Google dominates the search engine market share at a whopping 92%. SEO works by keyword ranking from search engines and depends on the algorithms of these search engines. SEO has its limitations. But based on the data, using a combination of well-planned SEO and good SMO, you can attain quite a bit of reach and traffic.
